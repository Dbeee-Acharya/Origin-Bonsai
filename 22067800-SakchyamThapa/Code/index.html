Large Bonsai

The Imperial bonsai is the largest and most majestic of all Bonsai trees. They grow between sixty and eighty inches tall and are found most often in the Japanese imperial gardens. They are also known as eight-handed due to the number of people required to move them


Medium Bonsai


Medium bonsai. Trees 20-50cm in height. Shohin : Small bonsai. Trees smaller than 20cm tall. Those smaller than 10cm may also be called mini bonsai.


Miniature Bonsai


a miniature plant (as a tree) grown in a pot by special methods to restrict its growth. also : the art of growing such a plant.
