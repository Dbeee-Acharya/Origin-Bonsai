Plant Details

Boxwood is a type of evergreen shrub or small tree that is commonly used in landscaping and horticulture. It is prized for its dense, green foliage and ability to be trimmed and shaped into a variety of forms. There are many different species of boxwood, including English boxwood (Buxus sempervirens), Korean boxwood (Buxus sinica), and Japanese boxwood (Buxus microphylla). Boxwood plants are popular choices for hedges, topiaries, and formal gardens, and are often used as a border plant or for foundation plantings. They are relatively low maintenance and can tolerate partial shade, but do best in well-draining soil and full sun. Boxwood plants are also popular as bonsai subjects.
