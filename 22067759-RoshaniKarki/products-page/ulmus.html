Plant Details

The Ulmus parvifolia, also known as the Chinese Elm, is a popular choice for bonsai enthusiasts due to its attractive, elm-like leaves and sturdy branches. This tree is native to China and Japan and is well-suited to life as a bonsai due to its ability to withstand pruning and shaping. Here are a few tips for caring for your Ulmus parvifolia bonsai: Provide your bonsai with plenty of indirect sunlight, as it needs at least 4-6 hours of sunlight per day to thrive. Water your bonsai regularly, being sure to keep the soil evenly moist but not waterlogged. Fertilize your bonsai tree with a balanced bonsai fertilize every month during the growing season. Prune your bonsai regularly to maintain its desired shape and encourage new growth. With proper care, your Ulmus parvifolia bonsai should thrive and bring a touch of natural beauty to your home or garden.
