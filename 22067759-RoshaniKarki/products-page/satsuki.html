Plant Details

The Satsuki azalea is a popular choice for bonsai enthusiasts because of its beautiful, fragrant flowers and lush, green foliage. This type of azalea is native to Japan and is known for its showy, pink blooms that appear in the springtime. The Satsuki azalea is a bit more challenging to care for than some other bonsai plants, as it requires a specific set of growing conditions in order to thrive. Here are a few tips for caring for your Satsuki azalea bonsai: Provide your bonsai with plenty of indirect sunlight, as it needs at least 4-6 hours of sunlight per day to bloom properly. Keep the soil evenly moist, but be careful not to overwater, as the azalea is susceptible to root rot. Fertilize your bonsai tree with an acidic bonsai fertilize every month during the growing season. Prune your bonsai regularly to maintain its desired shape and encourage new growth. With proper care, your Satsuki azalea bonsai should thrive and produce beautiful, pink blooms each spring.
