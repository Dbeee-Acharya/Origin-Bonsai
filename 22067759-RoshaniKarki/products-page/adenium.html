White Adenium Plant

Plant Details

The Adenium plant, also known as the Desert Rose, is a popular bonsai plant due to its unique, succulent-like appearance and striking flowers. This plant is native to the arid regions of Africa and is well-suited to life as a bonsai due to its ability to withstand prolonged periods of drought. Adenium plants come in a variety of colors, including white. Here are a few tips for caring for your Adenium bonsai plant: Provide your bonsai with plenty of sunlight, as it needs at least 6 hours of direct sunlight per day to thrive. Water your bonsai sparingly, as it is adapted to living in dry conditions. Allow the soil to dry out completely between waterings, and be careful not to overwater, as the Adenium is susceptible to root rot. Fertilize your bonsai tree with a balanced bonsai fertilize every month during the growing season. Prune your bonsai regularly to maintain its desired shape and encourage new growth. With proper care, your Adenium bonsai should thrive and produce beautiful, white flowers.
